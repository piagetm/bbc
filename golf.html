Tom Fazio-designed Par 72 Course

  • Course
  • Tom Fazio
  • The Fazio Philosophy

Our members only Par 72 golf course, designed by Tom Fazio, plays slightly more than 7,327 yards from the back tees and features dramatic water views from 11 of the 18 holes.

Recognized by his peers as one of the world's preeminent golf course designers, Tom Fazio participates in only the finest projects. Mr. Fazio has personally spent many hours on-site at Baker's Bay crafting the golf course's features and contours.

In creating his courses, Mr. Fazio honors a commitment to quality as well as the unique character and natural beauty of each setting. He gracefully melds the golf course into the natural terrain to give golfers of all abilities an exciting challenge and a sense of tradition. The Baker's Bay course perfectly reflects the Fazio philosophy.
